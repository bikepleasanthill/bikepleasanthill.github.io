About

Bike Pleasant Hill is a non-profit social organization founded in 2019 as a local working group of Bike East Bay.

Our vision is to create a comfortable and safe environment for bicyclists of all ages and abilities in the city of Pleasant Hill, California. We want to safely and easily bike and walk from our neighborhoods to local businesses, schools, and parks.

We provide free services to bicyclists and advocate for the construction of modern spaces for bicyclists and pedestrians.
